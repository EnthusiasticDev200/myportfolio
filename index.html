Current Project: Salon Management System

Goal: To streamline salon appointment scheduling and communication between customers and stylists.

Key Features:

  • In-Person & Online Booking – Customers can visit the salon or book appointments in advance with their preferred stylist.
  • Stylist Notifications – Every appointment triggers a notification to the selected stylist in real time.
  • Instant Response System – Stylist’s acceptance or rejection is instantly returned to the customer.
  • Secure Authentication – Implemented JWT and Bcrypt for authentication and password encryption.
  • Real-Time Communication – Integrated WebSocket for live appointment updates.
  • Email Notifications – Nodemailer sends appointment confirmations and reminders.
  • Form Validation – Used Express-Validator for backend data integrity.

Upcoming Features:

  • Home service booking
  • Online payment integration

Tech Stack:

Node.js | Express.js | MySQL | JWT | Nodemailer | WebSocket | Express-Validator | Bcrypt | Dotenv | Git
